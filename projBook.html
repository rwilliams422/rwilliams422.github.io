Writing and Editing a Children's Book

Date March 2020 - Current

Project Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Canva, Indesign

Abstract: At the beginning of the pandemic, I began and a project for writing and publishing a children’s book. While the publishing portion is still under production, the book itself is done. This project took attention to detail and editing skills to get the images and text correct. I wrote the story and commissioned my sister to paint the story I had written.

The book, One Big Crazy Family tells the story of my family as we grew larger and larger throughout my childhood. If you’ve ever wondered what being a part of a big family is like or if you’re apart of a big family this book is for you.
