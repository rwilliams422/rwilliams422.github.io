About Me

I am Professional and Technical Writing major at Saginaw Valley State University. I love to write news stories for SVSU’s school paper – The Valley Vanguard and develop my skills.

Some of my skills include organization, writing, and time management. I enjoy incorporating all of these things into both my professional life and my hobbies. In my professional life I have worked at public library, as a development intern and two churches. During these three jobs, I have managed events and people, written children’s curriculum, designed countless flyers, basic web development, and learned the importance of good directions.

For fun, I enjoy running, playing the piano and learning programming languages. I love finding hobbies that challenge me intellectually and that I can learn from. I also enjoy playing rugby for SVSU's Women's Club Rugby Team.
